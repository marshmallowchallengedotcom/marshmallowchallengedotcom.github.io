About

Mission

The Marshmallow Challenge Blog is a website dedicated to following startup trends, methodologies, and everything involved in running or creating startups.

About Me

Marshmallow challenge is a design exercise that helps teams to recognize the importance of prototyping. It demonstrates a very different way of thinking about product development and allows people to experience the power of iterative development in a highly simplified form.

I came across this exercise as a final-year high school student, and I realized how much this way of thinking could help any creative project. I’ve decided to focus my career on agile, fresh, and startups and small companies. As part of my job, I follow many aspects of startup projects.

On this blog, I’d like to share my findings with others, hoping that I might help a great idea become a reality and maybe change the world.

Thank You For Being Here

I love to discuss and learn anything in this domain, so please reach out to me if you have some suggestions or even just a cool idea you’d like to share.
