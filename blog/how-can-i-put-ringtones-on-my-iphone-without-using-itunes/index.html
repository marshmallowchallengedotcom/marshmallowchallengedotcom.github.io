How To Make Custom Ringtones For iPhone Using WALTR PRO

One of the great things about the iPhone is that you can customize it to suit your taste.

One way to do this is to create custom ringtones for your contacts. While you could use one of the many ringtone apps available, a more reliable and user-friendly solution is to use WALTR PRO.

This program makes it easy to transfer any audio or video file from your computer to your iPhone without needing to use iTunes.

In addition, WALTR PRO automatically converts the file into a format compatible with the iPhone, so you don’t have to worry about any conversion issues.

To create a custom ringtone, connect your iPhone to your computer and launch WALTR PRO.

Then, drag and drop the audio or video file you want to use onto the program’s interface.

Once the file has been transferred, it will automatically be converted and added to your ringtone library.

You can select it as your default ringtone or assign it to a specific contact.

How To Add Ringtones To Your iPhone With WALTR PRO

There are a few different ways to add ringtones to your iPhone, but one of the easiest is to use WALTR PRO.

This software makes it quick and easy to transfer ringtones from your computer to your iPhone and is compatible with all major file formats. Here’s how to get started:

Choose An Audio File

The first step is choosing an audio file you want to use as a ringtone. This can be any audio file, including MP files, MPA files, and MPR files.

If you have an iPhone with iOS 11 or later, you can also use HEIC files. Select the file you want to use and click “Open.”

Connect Your iPhone

Next, you’ll need to connect your iPhone to your computer using a Lightning cable. Once your device is connected, WALTR PRO will automatically detect and display it in the main window.

Transfer The Ringtone File

Once your iPhone is connected, drag and drop the audio file you want to use as a ringtone into WALTR PRO. The file will automatically be transferred to your device.

You can also click the “Add Files” button and select the file from your computer.

Once the file has been transferred, it will be available as a ringtone on your iPhone.

That’s all there is to it! With WALTR PRO, you can easily add custom ringtones to your iPhone without using iTunes.
