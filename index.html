Marshmallow Challenge

The Marshmallow Challenge is a simple design challenge for teams. The task is very simple: teams must construct the tallest structure by using: a few sticks of spaghetti, some tape, some string, and a single marshmallow that needs to be placed on the top.

The exercise helps teams to experience simple but essential lessons in team work, and creativity.
