Best Startup Companies

Jordan Cox Mar 25th, 2021

2020 has seen many disruptions that have destroyed lives and businesses. It has changed our way of living, working, and interacting with each other. However, with unexpected challenges human creativity thrives and results in new ideas which help us to overcome them.

This article reveals the best startup companies that have performed great, attracting a lot of attention, new employees, and investments.

The last year highlighted the importance of going online for every business. Many new ideas focus on remote solutions and to help with work from home, online education, and robotic delivery.

Some major trends from earlier years continue to be popular; many new tech startups focus on artificial intelligence, data science, and fintech solutions.

As an addition, in 2021 new companies need to pay attention to the sustainability, environmental, and social impact of their business.

Check our article about how startups are shaping themselves in 2021 for more information.

So, here is the list of the best startups everybody should know in 2021:

Capacity

Capacity is a new kind of helpdesk, powered by artificial intelligence, that automates support for your customers and employees.

Capacity continuously learns from organizational knowledge and the interactions within your business to automate your helpdesk, processes, and decisions in real time.

They have received $23.2 million funding from equity.com.

Streem

Streem is on a mission to make the world’s expertise accessible to all.

For example their solution allows the customer to use AR and show around the flat to the mechanics who will install some kitchen appliance.

They have received $10.5 million funding from Flying Fish Partners.
